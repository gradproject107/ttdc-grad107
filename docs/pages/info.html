TTDD展’22｜22nd Taiwan Tech Department of Design Graduation Show 2022


Taiwan Tech Design Graduation Show exhibits 69 design works featuring 106 graduates. Practices include product design, visual design, multimedia design, speculative design, etc. The issues we focus span across the modern society and into the future, guiding audiences to traverse between reality and fiction. Through expanding the boundaries of design, provoking discussions and interdisciplinary practices, we question and reimagine the nature of design.
