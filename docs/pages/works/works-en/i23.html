New Experiences in Gift Boxes


Looking at the New Year gift boxes on the market every year, it is found that most of them are repeatedly produced in similar styles. Thus, this project focuses on enhancing connection and strengthening the interactive experience between gift boxes and users, as result of increasing the fun of play.

As for gift boxes of the Dragon Boat Festival, we hope to deconstruct rice balls with different characteristics and use an analogy. Hence, consumers can better understand the special ingredients of different rice balls in different regions while eating the delicious rice balls, which are entertaining and educative.

Software Illustrator & Photoshop & Rhino, Paper, Rope, UV Printing, Light Curing, Spray Painting
H360 x W300 x D85mm, H100 x W100 x D100mm

TYPE Product Design, Graphic Design, Package Design
