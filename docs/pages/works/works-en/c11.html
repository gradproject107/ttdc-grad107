Bumble Trouble


Some of our habits and reactions to certain things can be traced back to our childhood experiences. If we try to change our perspective, the habits that you don’t like at first may eventually turn out to be your strengths.

The girl was bullied in her childhood, which led to her superpower ---- Bee Attack!

Having the power of The Bees, she thought the fear of being bullied no longer existed. However, she didn’t know that it would also cause her to lose friends.

Now, even her last friend is being attacked by The Bees, but how can she make it all stop?
