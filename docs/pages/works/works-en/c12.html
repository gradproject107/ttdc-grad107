Taste of Taiwanese Life


I am a foreign student who have come to Taiwan for four years. I have experienced a lot of cultural shock when I first arrived to Taiwan. Among them, the food culture reflects the unique taste of Taiwan. I hope to bring this culture to foreigners who come to Taiwan to visit through board games. Also promote the exchange of people from all over the world.
Taking Taiwan as the background, it reflects the local diverse food culture. In this unique place, players will encounter different foods and related questions in the game. Correctly name these foods and answer the questions to get the most points. The most Taiwanese foreigner!
