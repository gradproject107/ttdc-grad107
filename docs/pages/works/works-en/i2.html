WHISPER


Whisper uses a directional sound effect and lights to keep individuals refreshed when they are at home for an extended amount of time. Whisper features two sound modes that are adjusted by the movable speaker mechanism: showering and focusing. Showering mode gives an immersive sound impression for multiple users, while focusing mode is for single person separated sound. Depending on the quantity of users, users can rotate Whisper and enjoy the music.
