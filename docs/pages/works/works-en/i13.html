PITHINESS


"Pith paper plant" is a rare material and has great cultural significance of Taiwan. However, this plant is gradually forgotten over time, and the chance to apply it to actual use is getting fewer as well. Therefore, we want to know how we can make it move with the times and let people know more about these cultural stories behind it.

We keep exploring this material, looking into its production methods to put it into practice. By using it, we add embellishments to our daily life. We hope that people can experience it with touching rather than watching because by this way, you may have unexpected thought-provoking surprises to your senses.
