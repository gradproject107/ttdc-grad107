4 PEOPLE 1 NEKO


Based on various decision-making between interpersonal relationships, 4 PEOPLE 1 NEKO constructs a new worldview which includes four storylines: "Strawberry Girl," "Heartless Bullet," "Express Bus," and "Passerby." These four chapters can be taken as individual stories and animated by the AR application. Meanwhile, the four chapters can comprise a whole plot that shows how attitudes toward interpersonal relationships affect one another.

Illustrator, After Effects, Unity, Print, Paper
Poster: H594 ✕ W841 mm, Key Visual: H1682 ✕ 594 mm, AR Animation: 1min
