Reunion


Due to an accident in childhood, protagonist became a people-pleaser, do good turns for everyone to make up for his sense of guilt.Till a fatal car accident brought him to the nether world, he was forced to face his regret to affection and his private needs.Suddenly, an opportunity push him into a quandary…

Plot focus on affection between protagonist and his grandma, story background adapted from folklore religion, and the scenes based on Taiwanese old style interior.The dubbing spoke in Taiwanese Hokkien.Hopes to create a fantasy world with Taiwanese folklore, and a work full of human touch.

Software/
Adobe After Effect, Adobe Premiere, Clip studio, Procreate, Blender
1920 × 1080 px
