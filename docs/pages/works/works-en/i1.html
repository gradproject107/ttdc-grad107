HOMEET


The coronavirus has made Work From Home the new normal. Imaging the future working scenarios, HOMEET is a window that can create a connection between home and the world. By knocking, waving, writing, and many more, people can experience the existence of each other and interact easily. With HOMEET, more diverse social interactions will be innovated and people can embrace an immersed working atmosphere from home.

LCD Screen, Polyester, Leather, UV Curable Resin, Spray Paint, Acrylic
H1398 x W825 x D110 mm, H680 x W395 x D85 mm

TYPE Product Design, Interactive Design
