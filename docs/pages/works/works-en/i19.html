CARBEAR


CARBEAR is a transportation system that provides shared skateboards and shared scooters at amusement parks. In our experience of visiting amusement parks such as Mucha Zoo, Disney Universal Studios, etc., we found that the scope of these amusement parks is very large, but those tourists were often unable to complete the journey due to physical and time constraints. Therefore, we want to use this point-to-point shared transportation system to enhance the tourists' better visiting experience.

Aluminum alloy, Metal, SLA Resin, PU Tires
