Eatwow


Animal husbandry has contributed a lot to greenhouse gas emissions and land pollution. Because of that, in recent years, insect, which is rich in nutritional value, have attracted a lot of attention as a sustainable alternative to meat.

We found that human's fear of insects is mostly caused by existing stereotypes. Therefore, we want children to learn more about edible insects through Food and Agriculture Education, in which they can experience the potential of insects as food and break the stereotype that "insects are not suitable for food" by reducing psychological discomfort through bowl-shaped insect rearing equipment.

*There are risks of allergy from consuming edible insects. Please eat at your own risk.
