Omni Folder


People spend a lot of time looking for and trying cosmetics to have a perfect look. In the process, many unsuitable cosmetics are forgotten or thrown away.

We hope everyone can find their perfect look, thus we design the "Omni Daily Beauty Folder". "Omni" means "all-purpose" in Latin. Namely, it applies to any occasion, any place, any time.

With a series of disposable cosmetics, we create a unique cosmetic system reducing cosmetic switching burdens. Paired with stylish and portable leather clips, everyone can show their best selves in any style and occasion.

Leather, paper, aluminium, cosmetics, electroplating, SLA photosensitive resin, digital direct spray, PP plastic sheet
H67 x W110 x D27mm, H52 x W30 x D7mm, H46 x W9 x D6mm
