Tanrei Gothic


If Hanzi gothic typefaces had a different origin, is it possible that it was developed independently from western influences? This work is based on this hypothesis, through studying traditional Han Clerical scripts, an alternative path of developing gothic typefaces is proposed and realized into a modern typeface family.

Glyphs, Python, Pen Plotter, Stone Engraving
200 glyphs, 9 weights, 1 variable axis

TYPE Type Design
DEPARTMENT Commercial Design

Zhang, Wei-Zhong Zhang, Wei-Zhong is a Taiwanese student majoring in design at National Taiwan University of Science and Technology. He focuses on studying Hanzi and Latin type design, and incorporating the knowledge into his practice.
