Spin Control


In an era dominated by popularity, internet celebrities are idolized, and their values are decided by followers. When the judgement of rights and wrongs are manipulated by spin control, does the truth still matter?
This work depicts how two internet celebrities fell out with each other on plagiarism. While the followers overwhelmingly supported the more popular one and blindly attacked the other, they had no idea that the whole incident was actually staged by both of them.
