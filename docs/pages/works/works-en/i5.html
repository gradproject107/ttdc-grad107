Seda : Listen to the Echo of Nature


SEDA aims to bring the mesmerizing beauty of nature indoors. By extracting the essence of fascinating natural sceneries and using household appliances as the carrier, we combine the pure memory of nature with home experience. The elegant-shaped leaves that gather moisture in the air and condense it into dew. The burning red charcoal that warm palms in the forest. Waterfalls that pour down and create silver mist. When you gaze into all of these, time seems to freeze with serenity and vastness. SEDA wants to present the essence of nature in modern household appliances by capturing the sudden precious moment in nature.


TYPE Product Design, Home Appliance Design,
