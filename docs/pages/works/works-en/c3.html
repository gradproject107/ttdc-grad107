Deleted Scenes


This project deeply dug into several unaccomplished incidents related to graphic design since 1960 in Taiwan; it gives the auteur the ability to travel through time and space, fulfill these works through reproducing conditions at the time, so as to deliberate over power structure between specters of history and contemporary typography.

Hands, Macintosh, Print
W1040 × H760 mm(11 sheets)

TYPE Graphic Design
DEPARTMENT Commercial Design
