Plaper


This is a fully recyclable paper tableware set with a stable structure formed by three components fixed with each other with uniquely designed buckles. It allows the user to hold the cup, plate and fork simply, giving them a sense of calm while participating in any event, and even reducing the cost of recycling.

In this design ,it is easy to store and transport as a single piece of paper. Using non-coated paper as the only material, which reduces the amount of paper wasted in the recycling process, since the separation of coating is no longer necessary.

CircuWell non-coated paper
H80 x W300 x D420mm
