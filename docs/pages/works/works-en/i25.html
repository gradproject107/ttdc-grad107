FisherBox


Fishing is a recreational activity where one can enjoy being close to nature, however, many people are afraid to try this activity because they don’t have any experience and they don’t know anyone experienced enough to accompany them. Those who want to try fishing might also face troubles, as currently there are many kinds of tools sold in the market, they don’t know which to buy nor how to use them.

FisherBox is a trial pack designed for those who want to try fishing as a hobby but want to keep it simple, in hopes newbies can overcome their troubles.

Galvanized Iron, TPU, Nylon, Polyester, EVA foam, PE
H430 x W310 x D210~240mm

DEPARTMENT Industrial Design
