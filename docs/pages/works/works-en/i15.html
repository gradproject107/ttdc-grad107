YIYI｜Home Interactive Relaxation Companion


The epidemic makes it difficult for people to separate their work and life, which causes a lot of anxiety and panic. Therefore, we designed "YIYI", a perfect companion to help people relax in their busy lives. With beautiful motion and light effects slow down over time, it helps people calm down breathing and relieve stress. In addition, people can customize their own settings with the APP to make it easier for the body and mind to enter a state of relaxation. Stopping for a moment and interacting with YIYI will make people forget worries and enjoy the fun of life!

UV Curable Resin, ABS, Sensor, Stepper Motor, LED
H500 x W315 x D130 mm

TYPE Product Design, Interactive Design
DEPARTMENT Industrial Design
