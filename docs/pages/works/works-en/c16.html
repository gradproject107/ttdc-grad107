Canopy Gazing


When it comes to trees, what comes to your mind? The dappled shade beneath trees, or the tree canopy you have to gaze upward? Canopy Gazing uses patterns to demonstrate the " Symphony of Difference" of everything on the tree canopy, comprised of light and shadow art, hoping to bring the audience more imagination about the tree.
