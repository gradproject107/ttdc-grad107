Walking Birds: Boundary of Identity


Identity is revealed in action, it is what we do that determines who we are. When people's identity can no longer be categorized by current nationalities or ethnicities, and they no longer feel where they belong, they turn to learning from animals and making them a new category, by imitating the migratory behavior of migrating birds in search of companionship, making animals a nationality can choose. Through cross-species identity transformation, people break through the boundaries that limit identity.
