The Oceanic Lumiere


The ocean covers most of the earth's surface, but humans know little about it. According to the research, three-fourths of sea creatures can glow but few people know that. Therefore, we design a book that introduces luminous marine creatures, which brings readers to this mysterious and stunning inner space.
To simulate the experience of undersea exploration, this book contains an interactive structure and luminous printing, making readers dive into this luminous world. The field guide can be used at the same time as well. Readers can recognize these amazing creatures while exploring.

Books, Software (Procreate, Illustrator), Silk Screen Printing With Luminous Ink, Paper, Electronic Device
Book size 18 ✕ 18 cm, 60 ✕ 18 cm while open

TYPE Graphic Design, Book Design
