Textile Recycling Station


Nowadays, people tend to get rid of their clothing more frequently in the wake of fast fashion. However, existing textile recycling processes are too overloaded to digest these large quantities of clothes, which places a heavy burden on our earth.

By establishing explicit recycling standards, Textile Recycling Station guides people to separate clothes that are practically new or not, thus entering respective recycling processes. It can make the most of used clothing, speed up circulation, and prevent producing textile waste.

Arduino UNO, Adobe XD, PC plastic
H1120 x W550 x D550mm x 2pcs
